What We Treat

Services

Pain management and medication-assisted treatment rooted in evidence, delivered with compassion. We treat the whole person — not just the diagnosis.

Pain Management

Chronic pain conditions we treat

We offer structured, individualized treatment plans for a wide range of chronic pain conditions. Our goal is not just pain reduction — it's helping you regain function and get back to what matters.

01

Chronic Pain Management

Long-term, comprehensive management of persistent pain conditions. We work with you to build a sustainable treatment plan that fits your life — one focused on function, not just symptom suppression.

  • Chronic Pain
  • Generalized Pain Syndrome
  • Complex Regional Pain
02

Back & Neck Pain

Evaluation and management of spinal pain conditions, including cervical and lumbar issues. We review your history and imaging to develop an appropriate treatment approach.

  • Lower Back Pain
  • Neck Pain
  • Degenerative Disc Disease
  • Radiculopathy
03

Fibromyalgia

Structured management for fibromyalgia — a condition that is often undertreated or dismissed. We take fibromyalgia seriously and develop tailored plans that acknowledge the full complexity of this diagnosis.

  • Fibromyalgia
  • Widespread Pain
  • Fatigue-Related Pain
04

Neuropathy

Evaluation and management of neuropathic pain — the burning, shooting, or tingling pain caused by nerve damage. We identify contributing factors and work to reduce nerve pain and improve daily function.

  • Peripheral Neuropathy
  • Diabetic Neuropathy
  • Nerve Pain
05

Headache Management

Ongoing management for patients with chronic or recurrent headaches. We evaluate patterns, triggers, and contributing conditions to develop a proactive treatment plan.

  • Chronic Headaches
  • Migraines
  • Tension Headaches
06

Medication Management

Safe, compliant oversight of pain medications for patients already on a pain regimen. Includes regular follow-ups, medication review, and urine drug screening as appropriate.

  • Medication Review
  • Compliance Monitoring
  • Urine Drug Screen (in-office)
Medication-Assisted Treatment

MAT & Suboxone Program

Four Peaks Medical offers medication-assisted treatment (MAT) for opioid use disorder — including Suboxone (buprenorphine/naloxone). MAT is an FDA-approved, evidence-based treatment that reduces cravings and withdrawal, and supports long-term recovery.

We approach MAT without judgment. Opioid use disorder is a medical condition, and patients seeking help deserve the same respect and care as any other patient.

  • Suboxone / buprenorphine treatment
  • Structured, compliant treatment plans
  • Opioid use disorder evaluation
  • Ongoing medication management
  • In-person and telehealth options
  • Referral required to start
