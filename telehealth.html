Virtual Care

Telehealth

Four Peaks Medical offers virtual appointments for eligible patients. Same quality of care — no commute required.

How It Works

Care from wherever you are

Telehealth appointments at Four Peaks Medical work the same as in-person visits — you'll meet with Brooke Montgomery, FNP-B via a secure video connection. Your care plan, prescriptions, and follow-up are all handled the same way.

A smartphone, tablet, or computer With a working camera and microphone
A stable internet connection WiFi or cellular data works fine
A private, quiet space For a comfortable, confidential visit
Your medication list and ID Same documentation as an in-person visit

Eligibility

Is telehealth right for you?

Telehealth is available for many visit types at Four Peaks Medical. Some visits may require in-person attendance. Call us and we'll let you know what's right for your situation.

Telehealth may be suitable for

  • →Established patient follow-ups
  • →Medication management visits
  • →MAT follow-up appointments
  • →Patients with limited mobility or transportation
  • →Routine check-ins

Important to know

  • —You must be located in Delaware at time of visit
  • —Some visit types may require in-person
  • —Insurance coverage for telehealth varies by plan
  • —Call to confirm eligibility before scheduling
